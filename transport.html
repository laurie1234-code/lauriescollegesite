Here you will find some information on our on site transport at the festival.


An accessible buggy service operates short distance drop offs in a loop stopping at the accessible campsite and the entrance to the arena.


Only you and your essential companion will be able to us this service, subject to availability - note that we cannot enter the main arena due to safety concerns.
