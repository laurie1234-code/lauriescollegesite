Essential Companions

An essential companion ticket is provided to customers whom would be unable to attend without a companions support or help. If your Nimbus Access Card or Electric Picnic Digital Access Pass details your need for an essential companion, a ticket will be provided free of charge as part of your application.

Your essential companion must be aged 18 or over and be willing and able to perform all the requirements needed of an essential companion.

If you need different companions to assume the role of care for you during the festival please indicate this on the Electric Picnic Accessible Facilities Application.

Guide Dogs

Assistance/Guide Dogs can be approved in accordance with your requirements indicated on your Nimbus Access Card or an Electric Picnic Digital Access Pass

A spending area will also be available for assistance dogs
