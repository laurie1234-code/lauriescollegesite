Team Statement 2025

We look forward to welcoming you back to Electric Picnic 2025. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all our attendees! The festival experience is at the core of our minds when catering to everyone at the festival.

Thats why we are committed to enhancing everyones experience to the best of our abilities. We take all requests for assistance and information incredibly seriously - if our attendees werent here we wouldnt be either! Following on with feedback recieved from our 2024 festival, we plan to bring forward your brilliant ideas to this years experience. Follow along with this website to get acquainted with what we have on offer for you.

We have focused on improving our accesibility services over the year for this years upcoming festival. This includes but is not limited to car park access routes, festival arena access routes, viewing spaces and platforms and our sensory calm spaces. In the following content pages you can find access to information on Medication, Companions, Grounds, Transport & a site map! A feedback form is also included - let us know what we can do better!
