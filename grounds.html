We have many accessibility driven facilities on our festival grounds!

Raised Viewing Platforms


Available for wheelchair users and those with restricted mobility, our raised viewing platforms can be approved for use in accordance with your requirements. Only approved customers and their companions are approved to use these facilities.


Please note that these are provided on a first come first serve basis.



Ground Viewing Areas


Ground Viewing areas are also provided for those who need a less crowded space and the option of sitting for short periods of time.


Similar to the raised viewing platforms, you may attend this area with your designated companion alongside the area being on a first come first served basis.



Toilets and Changing Areas


Standard and Wheelchair accessible toilets are located for accessibility customers across the event location.


We maintain a changing area with an adult changing bed, toilet sink and hoist. Please note that we cannot provide hoist slings, and to remember to bring your own!



Accessible Entrance Lanes


There are dedicated Entrance lanes for our accessibility customers.



Bars & Info


Similarly, we have dedicated accessibility lanes for access to bars, with a lowered counter and a system that bypasses the main queuing setup.


There is an information hub manned by our dedicated team especially designed for our accessibility customers, that is located in the main arena.
