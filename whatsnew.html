This is my about page for my Electric Picnic Accesibility Website. I created this website as a guide for users to obtain information on the accesibility focused features of Electric Picnic. I moddeled the piece off of a official EP document that provides information on the events accesibility facilities (you can field access to this by clicking HERE.

I focused on maintaining a similar green color scheme, that is easy on the eyes and useful for text definition for those whom are visually impaired. I have included audio read throughs of each page just above the footer to help with this too. The site gives insight into different accesibility facilities and information at the festival.

The site focuses on fitting the profile for what would be an accesibility focused site for Electric Picnic. I have inputted information from their official accesibility information page, including a feedback form, information tables, alt text, images and have provided external links to the official site in the footer.
